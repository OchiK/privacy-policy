## Overview

A Minimalist Editorial aesthetic tailored for legal trust and clarity. It merges the structure of a high-end print publication with the clarity required for a software privacy statement. It prioritizes content hierarchy, whitespace, and precise lines over background gradients and shadows.

## Colors

The color palette is rooted in limestone/cream neutrals in light mode, deep charcoal in dark mode, and a soft forest green for trust signals.

- **Primary Light Background (#f7f5f0):** Soft warm limestone/cream that is gentler on the eyes than pure white.
- **Secondary Light Background (#ffffff):** Crisp white used for main body cards to contrast with the warm background.
- **Primary Dark Background (#090a0c):** Deep charcoal ink background.
- **Secondary Dark Background (#111318):** Low-contrast dark slate card background.
- **Accent Light (#15803d):** Forest Green for trust signals, links, and checks.
- **Accent Dark (#22c55e):** Muted Emerald/Green for trust states.
- **Borders:** Thin, elegant hairlines.

## Typography

Typography is the core of this design. It pairs a characterful serif for headings with a clean sans-serif for reading comfort.

- **Headings (Lora):** A contemporary serif with brushed curves that conveys authority and editorial care.
- **Body (Inter):** Highly legible sans-serif optimal for reading legal disclosures.
- **Utility (Monospace):** Used for version history, dates, or contact items.

## Layout

A centered single-column layout with generous vertical margins (`64px` on desktop) and a clean floating header banner. Content reads like a legal brochure with fine border separators.

## Elevation & Depth

No deep dropshadows or heavy glass filters are used. Flat sheets and fine hairlines (`1px`) define boundaries to maintain print-like qualities.

## Shapes

Mild rounding (`8px` to `16px`) is used to soften the layout slightly, but it avoids overly round or bubbly shapes.

## Components

- **Floating Header Banner:** A slim, centered, border-bound container that sits cleanly at the top of the viewport.
- **Content Cards:** Clean cards with a subtle solid border rather than a blurry shadow background.
- **Checklist Bullets:** Small square or rounded teal/green badge indicators that sit inline with text.
- **Email CTA Link:** A simple solid-background button that acts as the primary contact endpoint.

## Do's and Don'ts

- **DO** use thin, subtle borders to separate sections.
- **DO** maintain high text contrast (at least 4.5:1).
- **DON'T** use purple, pink, or orange gradients in backgrounds.
- **DON'T** use emojis as functional UI icons; use simple SVG outline icons instead.
- **DON'T** scale layout blocks on hover; use border color and background transitions.
